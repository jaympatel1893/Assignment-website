 "So what makes this fine art," you ask?

 Brad Young Photography understands that fine art is defined relative to its viewer.
Fine art can be something you simply admire because of how it makes you feel
when you look at it. Brad Young Photography defines fine art as a subject that
encourages the innate desire for creative fulfillment.

Envision the snow-capped mountain that stands proudly as it encourages awe-
struck gazes. Appreciate the loyal pet napping lazily in a warm chair. Relax as a
slow sunset takes a busy day away. No matter the subject matter, fine art
photography provokes the imagination we've long confined, expands our previously
padlocked perspectives, and stirs the emotions deep within... whatever they may
be.
